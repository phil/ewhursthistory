PEPPER MAKES MINT BETTER

Session Tracker Version 0.9

Installing Session Tracker
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Upload the /sessiontracker/ directory and its contents to 
/mint/pepper/beaucollins/. If the directory /beaucollins/ doesn't exist, create it.

2. Login to your Mint installation and in the Preferences click "Install" under
Pepper.

3. Click the Session Tracker "Install" button. Click "Okay."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Updating Session Tracker
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Upload the /sessiontracker/ directory and its contents to /mint/pepper/beaucollins/.
