Picture collection
We have a large collection of photographs of local places and events. Many of these have been copied from photographs lent by villagers. We are always interested in adding to our collection and if anyone has any photographs, postcards or 'memorabilia' (programmes, tickets etc.) that they would be prepared to let us copy we would be very grateful.
